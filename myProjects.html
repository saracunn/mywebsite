Systems Integration Group Project - Fall 2020

The purpose of this group project is to understand the steps and holistic approach towards the development and integration of an enterprise system. The group will evaluate and understand the strategic direction of the given organization at its core and document the foundations of what drives its purpose. The goal is to familiarize the group with both an external and internal process evaluation in order to provide a comprehensive view of all system components. Overall, the group should understand that the purpose of any system integration is to provide an organization with competitive advantage while leveraging the technology it already has or needs to obtain.
